M.S. Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as M.S. Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national cricket team. Dhoni is considered one of the greatest cricketing captains of all time, having won numerous awards and accolades for his leadership.

"I always believe that if you work hard, you will get what you deserve."

Under Dhoni's captaincy, the Indian cricket team won numerous titles, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. Dhoni is also known for his calm demeanor and excellent decision-making abilities, which have earned him the nickname "Captain Cool".

Dhoni is one of the most successful Indian cricketers of all time, with numerous records and achievements to his name. He is the first Indian wicket-keeper to score 4,000 test runs, and also holds the record for most stumpings in international cricket. In 2011, Dhoni was awarded the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award, India's highest honor for achievement
